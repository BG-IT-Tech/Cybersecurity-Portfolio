## About This Portfolio

This portfolio contains nine hands-on cybersecurity projects completed as part of my Security+ coursework. Each project uses a fictional company scenario (Nexarion Solutions) to demonstrate real-world application of Security+ concepts including threat response, risk management, endpoint hardening, and governance.

All scenarios, logs, and data are fictional and created for educational purposes only.

## What I Learned

Working through these projects gave me practical experience applying Security+ domains to realistic scenarios. I learned how to think like an analyst — not just identify problems, but prioritize them by risk and communicate findings clearly. The log analysis and incident response projects especially helped me understand how attacks unfold over time and why detection speed matters.
